Services We Provide

Those who qualify for these services:

    Must reside within Kent school zip code boundary.

    Those who currently are without permanent shelter.

Rent and Utility Funds

    You can obtain this once every calendar year.

    Your name must be the name on the bill submitted.

    The bill must be marked as urgent or be a shut-off notice.

Gas Vouchers

    You can obtain this once every six months.

    You must have a valid/current Driver's license (not an ID card).

Thrift Store Vouchers

    You can obtain this once every six months.

    This can be used for clothing and small household items.

    Thrift Store Hours: *TBA*

Obtaining a Driver's License or ID Card

    If you're seeking an ID card, check with DSHS to see if you qualify for a voucher (we'll pay for the difference).

    An appointment will be scheduled to meet with you at the Department of Licensing in Kent.

Emergency Food/Toiletries

    You can obtain this once every month.
